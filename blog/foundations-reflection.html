What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

I was definetely suprised at how much I expected myself to know and how fragile my ego was if I didnt know, I found myself stressing and making things worse by being negative if I didnt know things at the start but I have slowly learned to give myself a bit of credit for what I do know

I was also really suprised at how I managed the problem solving as when I was at uni I would have little meltdowns because I couldnt solve something but during this process I found myself pushing through and understanding that I am still learning and its about the journey not the destination

I also found implementing a Growth Mindset outlook has helped not only in study but all areas of my life im starting to look at things as opportunities and rather looking at downfalls as a bad thing I now look at it as a lesson that i needed to learn

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

From my perspective, I would say values define the interactions between you and your team and this can significcantly impact a groups dynamic in a positive or negative way. So knowing what values you bring to the team and knowing how to manage your less forth-coming characteristics is a must as it can allow a better dynamic for yourself and the group. Empathy plays a really big part in learning and programming as we are all human and we do make mistakes and tend to be rather negative about those mistakes, being able to have empathy towards yourself in times of stress or confusion or anger can hugely impact the dynamics of your learning as it allows you to not be so negative and push through and to recognise the amazing work you have already done. Another extremely important ingredient to the recipe is self-awareness as being self aware can help you recognise emotions that may effect your mood or outlook on the work you are currently doing. For example, when i run into an issue with my programming and start to get frustrated I can recognise and be able to take a break so I can relax for a second, being able to do this is so important to the learning process as it helps you not be negative and also helps you with getting burnt out less as well

What is the difference between Margin, Padding, and Border?

Margin: The basis of a Margin is to create space around the content that you desirre it too, this can be extremely useful as it helps you position things like nav bars and images to where you want. The margin allows us to do this as it is a property that exists outside of border and padding parameters which means we can use margin to create space from picture to picture or anything really. This is the main difference that margin has over padding and border because it allows us to manipulate the space between content and the positioning.

Padding: The basis of padding is to align and position content inside the box, this is how we identify the difference bewtween using margin and padding. Think of it like this when you have a house with a fence around and you want to make the fence smaller or bigger do you make changes to the fence(margin) or do you make the house(padding) bigger and create space in between the house and fence? that is how you should be viewing margin and padding as padding makes the "house" bigger and margin changes the space of the "fence".

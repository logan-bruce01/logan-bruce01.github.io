Identify Values

Your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

My five strengths from the VIA Character test is:
1. Kindness:

I think this will really help me as I can show kindness to other people when they need help with their work but also I can show kindness to myself when im frustrated or annoyed about an issue or problem that I am running into which will be very beneficial for my mindset going into this course

2. Fairness:

The way I perceive this is similar to the infamous quote of "treat other people how you want to be treated", where I will treat you with fairness and kindness in hopes that I will receive the same treatment back which will help my learning journey because it will help me with my soft skills and the core learning of this course

3. Forgiveness:

I feel forgiveness is a key attribute for anyone to have as their is no benfeits of holding grudges against poeople and showing judgement to other people for mistakes they have made. I feel this will effect my learning as I can show forgiveness to myself if I make mistakes and continue to proceed with my learning

4. Teamwork:

Teamwork is a really big attribute for me personally because I feel working in a team enviroment helps create this space of zen(you could call it) where everyone is collaborating with each other and it might not all be smooth sailing but there will be the ups and down but it also removes the pressure because you have other people to go to. For example, if you are running into an issue then you can ask one of your co-workers to help you with your problem. I also add an aspect of fun and enjoyment because your not by yourself, because it is human nature to want to be engaging with other people socially and to be around other people.

5. Judgement:

I think this value is key because it not judgement in the sense of why did you do this but how can we fix this and improve this, which I feel can bring a whole new perspective to a project and I think it will give me good grasp on my learnign techniques and work ethic as I can have judgement and dictate wether im doing something right or working on my course enough.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why?

When I was in uni, I had a group report that had to be done and obviously with it being in covid there was no way for us to meet in person and discuss ideas and work on the report. Because of this it created alot of tension because of the lack of communication and the lack of work being produced, I tried to assign certatin topics of the report to the people in the group and although the produced a small amount of work it was very minimal and you could tell that not alot of effort was produced obviously this created tension on my side of things as I wanted to pass, this lead to me asking them to redo their parts of the report which did not work. I think my strategy now would be to display my work or a standard of work that was appropriate to receive the grade we were after and also create a want on their side of things to receive a passing grade because this would have lead to them putting more effort and work.

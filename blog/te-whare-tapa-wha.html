Taha Tinana
Physical Wellbeing

  • Go to the gym 3-5 times a week
  • Go for a run 2-3 times a week
  • Drink 2-3L of water a day
  • Make sure to get a minimum of 7hrs of sleep



    • Taha Whānau
    Family Wellbeing

  • Check in on my family members every week
  • Create time to spend with my family once a week
  • Take more interest in whats going on in my siblings lives



    • Taha Hinengaro
    Emotional and Mental Wellbeing

  • Doing something I love once a day
  • Take time to think and meditate on things
  • Read a book for 10-15mins a day
  • Go for walk 1-2 times a day with my dog



    • Taha Wairua
    Spiritual Wellbeing

  • Meditate for 5-10 mins a day
  • Take time to get out into nature a few times a week



    • Whenua
    Interconnections to the land and environment

  • Once a week take time to go into nature
